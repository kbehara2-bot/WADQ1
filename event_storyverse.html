StoryVerse

Date: 30 September 2025

Venue: Open Auditorium

StoryVerse aimed to celebrate the joy of storytelling by offering participants a variety of imaginative, story-driven activities at different stations. Activities included:

  • Cover Canvas: Participants designed personalised book covers featuring titles, doodles and artwork inspired by favourite stories.
  • Plot Builders: Collaborative story-building where participants added sentences to story beginnings from various genres, producing unexpected narratives.
