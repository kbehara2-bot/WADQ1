Pen Pal Programme

Date: February - March 2025

Venue: Club-run correspondence (month-long)

The Pen Pal Programme ran across an entire month, during which participants anonymously exchanged letters. Members wrote thoughtful messages to unknown partners, and identities were revealed only during the final exchange, making the experience exciting and deeply personal.

This initiative grew far beyond expectations and attracted a large number of students across different departments. Thanks to the overwhelming participation and the heartwarming feedback we received, the Pen Pal Programme was officially selected as Bookscapeâ€™s flagship event.
