About Bookscape

Bookscape is the official book club of GITAM, created to bring together students who share a love for reading, storytelling, and meaningful discussions. The club provides a welcoming space where readers of all genres can connect, exchange ideas, and explore new books together.

We conduct monthly reading circles, creative discussions, and interactive events that encourage students to read more and share their perspectives. Bookscape also aims to build a vibrant reading culture on campus by hosting engaging activities and providing a supportive community for budding readers and writers.
