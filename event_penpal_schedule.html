Pen Pal Programme — Schedule & Details

Event Dates (Exchange Days):

  • 3rd, 4th March — 1st exchange
  • 10th, 11th March — 2nd exchange
  • 17th, 18th March — 3rd exchange
  • 24th March — Final exchange (reveal)

Venues (benches / exchange points):

  • KRC Lawn
  • Coke Station
  • Gandhi Park
  • Open Auditorium

Bookscape’s flagship Pen Pal Programme spans one month and involves anonymous letter exchanges between participants. Interested students register via a Google form and digitally sign event rules. Each participant is randomly assigned a pen pal; issuing and receiving of letters happens on designated exchange days under club supervision. Envelopes and seals are provided to add to the excitement and ensure privacy.
