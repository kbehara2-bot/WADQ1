Haunted Hunt

Date: 31 October 2025

Venue: Cokestation Road

Bookscape organised the Haunted Hunt as a Halloween riddle-based activity designed to bring joy and excitement to participants. Teams followed clues across the venue, solved puzzles and completed short challenges at each station.

The event was a success with more than 200 participants, seamless coordination by the Events team, and a lively, festive atmosphere that combined literature-inspired riddles with playful competition.
