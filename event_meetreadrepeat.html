Meet, Read, Repeat

Date: 23 October 2025

Venue: MBA Classroom

This initiative provided a relaxed, supportive environment designed as a peaceful break from daily routines. Students were invited to bring their own books — academic, fiction, or personal reads — and spend time reading in the shared presence of peers with a common interest.

The main purpose was to create a quiet, collective reading space that encouraged focused reading and promoted inner calm and well-being through a shared literary experience.
